ALWAYS KEEP FRESH WATER AVAILABLE. It is important for health and energy to maintain optimum hydration.

FEED A QUALITY DIET AND PREVENT OBESITY. In many ways, overweight can affect human and animal health. Follow your veterinarian's dietary recommendations based on size, age, activity level and breed and on your dog's nutritional needs. Recall offering safe care as bonuses rather than table scraps.

HAVE YOUR PET EXAMINED BY A VETERINARIAN ON A REGULAR BASIS. Your veterinarian will provide you with data on procedures for vaccination, deworming and prevention of external parasites. Keep in your home or with you a copy of your pet's vaccination records when you're traveling. When you think your pet might be sick, wounded, or if something just doesn't seem right, call your veterinarian. 

PROVIDE AMPLE OPPORTUNITIES TO EXERCISE. Make sure that your pet has the regular exercise necessary to make it healthy. The pet will be more able to participate in the activities it enjoys by being in shape.

COMMUNICATE WITH YOUR DOG AND DEVELOP A RELATIONSHIP. Dogs are social creatures who must communicate with their owners. Quality time will help you get to know your dog and appreciate the specific needs it may have, as well as strengthen the ability to recognize early signs of a disease that may develop. However, time spent cultivating a relationship can help to prevent other harmful patterns of behaviour.

TRAIN YOUR DOG TO FOLLOW THE SIMPLE COMMANDS. Training classes for puppies and dogs can be very useful. The more your dog follows simple and required orders, the greater the chances of enjoying a healthy and long life for your pet.

DENTAL CARE IS VERY IMPORTANT. Most species are susceptible to gum disease that can have serious consequences.

PRACTICE REPRODUCTIVE CONTROL. A certain choice is spaying or neutering if you do not wish to produce puppies. If, for other reasons, you plan to breed your dog or oppose spaying and neutering, take appropriate action to prevent discontent. Consult your veterinarian about other available options.
