Shih Tzu

They are little in size, and their nose, lips, and eyes depends on what dog color they have. Their height range is up to 11 inches and weighs for about 9-6 pounds (under 9 pounds are called imperial Shih Tzu). They are vulnerable in health problems like slipped stifle and spinal disc disease, and they also suffer from ear infections and eye problems. Lives about 15 years or more and their litter size is about 2-6 puppies. Shih Tzu needs a daily comb of bristle brush.
