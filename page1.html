Aspin / Asong Pinoy

Their average size is medium, fuzzy hair, and their skin can have different colors. They are the Philippines’ signature dog that are found in streets, but lately they are capturing our homes. Aspins are not a high maintenance breed that is will bring us joy. Aspins’ life span is about 10-14 years, litter size is about 2-6 puppies, and doesn’t need to be groomed luxuriously. Aspin is the abbreviated word of “asong pinoy”
